About AK Tutoring

AK Tutoring was founded with one goal: to make math more approachable and achievable for every student. With years of experience tutoring middle school through college-level math, we understand the challenges students face—and how to overcome them.

Whether you're preparing for a major test like the SAT or just trying to get through a tough class, our one-on-one sessions are tailored to your learning style, pace, and goals.
